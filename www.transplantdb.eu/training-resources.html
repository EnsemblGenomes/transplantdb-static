Training resources

transPLANT is running a series of training courses across Europe. The next one will be held at EMBL-EBI, Hinton, UK, 1st-3rd July, 2015. When registration is opened, details will be posted here. Meanwhile, you can access material from the previous transPLANT training courses here, including presentations, tutorials and worked examples for transPLANT tools, services and standards. Materials are grouped by (currently 6) transPLANT user training workshops and related events.
